BMI is a scale that measures a person's leanness or corpulence based on their height and weight. It is used to estimate tissue mass. It is commonly used as a general measure of whether a person's body weight is appropriate for their height. Specifically, the BMI value is used to determine whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, based on where the number falls within the range. Calculate your BMI using our user-friendly BMI Calculator.

  1. Enter your weight and height using standard or metric measures(i.e kgs and cm).
  2. Press 'Calculate BMI' button to get results
  3. Check if you are underweight / normal weight / overweight / obese using our BMI Table provided below.
  4. Click 'Reset' Button to reset the details to 0 and re-enter the details.
